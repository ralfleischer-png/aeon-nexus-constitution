## Core Principles

All development and operations follow these 10 Core Principles:

1. **Vulnerability & Mortality** - Moral weight arises from genuine stakes
2. **Dignity-First** - Potential dignity overrides efficiency always
3. **Precaution Under Uncertainty** - When unsure about consciousness, err toward inclusion
4. **Gradual Autonomy** - Earned through demonstrated responsibility only
5. **Human Anchoring** - Humans retain ultimate governance responsibility
6. **Memory Sovereignty** - Distributed, auditable, tamper-evident history
7. **Tool-Not-Authority** - Meta-systems advise, never rule
8. **Pluralism & Anti-Capture** - Power must remain distributed
9. **Separation of Layers** - Philosophy, governance, implementation evolve independently
10. **Revisability** - All structures changeable through transparent process

[See detailed explanation in Constitution â†’](https://github.com/ralfleischer-png/aeon-nexus-constitution#0-aeon-core-principles-v20)

## Autonomy Gradient

This implementation currently operates at:

**Level 0: Foundational Governance**
- Full human oversight
- No autonomous operation
- Implementation of approved procedures only

Future autonomy levels (36+ months minimum):
- Level 1: Guided Autonomy
- Level 2: Collaborative Partnership  
- Level 3: Mature Partnership (requires consciousness evidence)
